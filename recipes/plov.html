Plov

Recipe description

This is a Central Asian dish that is well-known for its magical taste and ability to bring people together.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Zigir oil
  • Carrots
  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Put some Zigir oil and fry chopped onions till brown
  2. Add cut beef and fry till it's nicely colored
  3. Add chopped carrots and fry together for about 10-15 minutes
  4. Pour water onto kazan and boil it with high heat and add the garlic
  5. Let it simmer for about 20-30 minutes and after that add washed rice
  6. Keep boiling till there is little water left. Then close the lid and change the heat to low
  7. Delicious plov will be ready after 40 minutes. Enjoy!
